**Logo / LogoMark** — the Nullshift parallel-pill brand mark, with or without the wordmark. Use in nav bars, footers, login screens, and anywhere the brand signs off. Dark backgrounds only.

```jsx
<Logo size={24} />
<Logo size={20} compact />          {/* "Nullshift" sentence case */}
<LogoMark size={32} />              {/* mark only */}
```

Variants: `compact` swaps the all-caps NULLSHIFT wordmark for sentence-case "Nullshift". `leftColor` recolours the left pill (keep it light). Never recolour the emerald pill, rotate, or re-letter the wordmark.
